About Us

Welcome to Mithai Ki Dukan! Founded in XYZ city in 1996, Mithai Ki Dukan has been a beloved name in the world of sweets for decades. What began as a small, family-owned shop has grown into a cherished brand with over 70 franchises across India. Our journey started with a simple mission: to bring the rich, diverse flavors of traditional mithai to every corner of our community. We take pride in our time-honored recipes, meticulously crafted with the finest ingredients and a touch of love. Our dedication to quality and authenticity has earned us a special place in the hearts of sweet enthusiasts everywhere. At Mithai Ki Dukan, every bite tells a story of tradition and taste. Whether you’re enjoying our classic sweets or exploring our innovative creations, we’re committed to delivering an experience that’s as delightful as it is delicious. Thank you for joining us on this sweet journey. We look forward to sharing many more moments of joy and sweetness with you!
